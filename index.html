At Caribbean Connection we provide you with the strongest possible platform to encourage your companies growth. Whether it’s a conference, a seminar, a holiday party or unique and custom experience, our team has the expertise to bring your vision to life. We work with you from the initial concept, planning and design, through to onsite management and production. We understand the investment of corporate events; we are thoughtful and considerate of each event and it’s goals. We can make your event and training seminar an unforgettable experience and your people effectively learn what you set them out to learn.


SERVICES

At Caribbean Connection, we are in a unique position to provide all of our services through our in house team; this ensures the highest quality of production and results in flawless events.
The service include, accommodation set-up, event venue contracting, seminar and speaker planning, hiring or execution. We also have predetermined seminars for the growth of your personnel in a completely set up for you learning environment. In our events you will learn the culture of the countries where we operate.


INTERNATIONAL EVENT PRODUCTION

Caribbean Connection was founded by industry and country experts who specialize in creating innovative, sophisticated and cutting edge events for you and your company in the Caribbean and Mexico. With many years of experience and deep knowledge of the countries where our events are held. We have developed a rich knowledge and understanding of the event, seminar, and hospitality worlds: resulting in our reputation for creating truly unforgettable moments. Our unique emphasis in the local culture and history of the Dominican Republic and Mexico will ensure that your company will learn effectively and come back with the changed mindset.
As a company we are storytellers, and coaches believing that the experience of the event can have a major impact on guests, it is our mission to ensure each and every event is thoughtfully produced and curated to create a moment that forges life long impressions.


STRATEGY ■ IDEATION ■ DESIGN ■ DEVELOPMENT ■ ACTIVATION
